## Kentucky LLC Search

**Before falling in love with a name, check availability:**

Kentucky Secretary of State Business Search:
https://apps.sos.ky.gov/land/businesssearch/

Search for:
- "Keystone" (see all variations)
- Exact name you want
- Similar names

**What to look for:**
- Exact match = ❌ Can't use
- Very similar = ⚠️ May cause confusion
- Different industry = ✅ Probably fine

## Google Play / App Store Considerations

Your **developer name** on app stores can be:
- Same as LLC name (clean, professional)
- Different from LLC name (flexible)

Example:
- **LLC:** Keystone Digital LLC
- **Play Store Developer:** "Keystone Apps" or "HomeBeacon by Keystone"

This gives you flexibility to keep app-facing name separate from legal entity.
