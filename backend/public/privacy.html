Privacy Policy - HomeBeacon

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Introduction

HomeBeacon ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our senior wellness monitoring application.

Information We Collect

HomeBeacon collects the following types of information:

Account Information

  • Email address (for account creation and notifications)
  • Display name (for family member identification)

Wellness Data

  • Activity data: Motion detection via device accelerometer (step count, activity levels)
  • Battery status: Device battery level and charging state
  • Screen time: General device usage patterns

We do NOT collect: Location data, photos, contacts, messages, or browsing history.

How We Use Information

  • Monitor senior wellness patterns
  • Send alerts to family members when concerning patterns are detected
  • Improve our service and user experience

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With family members you explicitly invite to your care network
  • As required by law or to protect our legal rights

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure password storage (bcrypt hashing)
  • Regular security audits

Data Retention

Wellness data is retained for 90 days. Account data is deleted within 30 days of account deletion request.

Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Download your wellness data

To exercise these rights, use the "Delete Account" feature in the app Settings or contact us at:

Contact Us

Email: Support@keystonedigitalcorp.com

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Children's Privacy

HomeBeacon is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
