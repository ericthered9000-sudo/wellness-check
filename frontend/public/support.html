Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a family member?

In the app, go to Settings and select "Invite Family Member." Enter their email address, and they'll receive an invitation to connect and view your wellness updates.

Is my data private?

Yes! You control what you share. Only the family members you invite can see your wellness information. We never sell your data or share it with third parties.

How does the emergency button work?

Press and hold the red emergency button for 2 seconds. A confirmation screen will appear. Tap "Call 911" to dial emergency services. The hold action prevents accidental calls.

Can I delete my data?

Yes. Go to Settings > Delete All Data. This will permanently remove all your wellness data from our servers.

Does this work without internet?

Most features work offline. Your check-ins and activity will sync when you reconnect to the internet.

Is this medical advice?

No. Wellness Check is for wellness tracking only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult healthcare professionals for medical decisions.

How do I change my theme?

Tap the theme button (color palette icon) in the top-right corner of the app. Choose from 6 themes: Default, Dark, Calm, Ocean, Forest, or Sunset.

How do medication reminders work?

Add your medications in the "Meds" tab. Set the name, dosage, and time for each medication. You'll receive push notifications when it's time to take each medication.

What is the wellness score?

The wellness score (0-100) is calculated from your daily activities: check-ins, medications taken, movement, and other factors. A higher score indicates more consistent wellness patterns.
