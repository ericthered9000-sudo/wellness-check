Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

Introduction

Wellness Check ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Wellness Data

  • Wellness check-in responses
  • Self-reported activity logs
  • Medication reminders and adherence
  • Doctor appointment schedules
  • Wellness scores (calculated from above data)

App Usage

  • App launch and session duration
  • Feature usage patterns
  • Crash reports (anonymous)

What We Do NOT Collect

  • GPS location data
  • Photos or videos
  • Contacts
  • SMS or call logs
  • Browsing history
  • Sensitive personal information (Social Security, financial, etc.)

How We Use Your Information

  • Display wellness information to you and your designated family members
  • Send medication reminders and check-in notifications
  • Calculate wellness scores
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on your device and synced to our encrypted servers. We use industry-standard HTTPS encryption for all data transmission.

  • At Rest: Data stored on our servers is encrypted
  • In Transit: All data transfers use HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • On Device: Local data is stored in your device's secure storage

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your data. We do NOT share your data with third parties except:

  • With family members you explicitly invite to view your wellness information
  • With service providers who help us operate the app (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • If required by law (e.g., legal subpoena)

Your Rights

Delete Your Data

You can delete all your data at any time by going to Settings > Delete All Data in the app.

Export Your Data

Contact us to receive a copy of your data in a portable format.

Withdraw Consent

You can revoke family member access to your wellness information at any time.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you use the app. When you delete your account, all data is permanently removed within 30 days.

Crash logs are retained for 90 days for debugging purposes.

Children's Privacy

Wellness Check is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Medical Disclaimer

Wellness Check is NOT a medical app. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult healthcare professionals for medical decisions.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
