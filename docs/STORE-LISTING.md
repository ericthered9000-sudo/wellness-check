## Full Description (4000 characters max)

```
HomeBeacon helps seniors stay independent while keeping their families connected and informed about their wellbeing.

🎯 SIMPLE CHECK-INS
One tap lets family know you're okay. No complicated menus or confusing interfaces.

📊 WELLNESS SCORE
A simple 0-100 score based on daily activity patterns helps families see overall health trends at a glance.

💊 MEDICATION REMINDERS
Never miss a medication. Track medications with reminders and adherence reporting.

📅 DOCTOR VISITS
Keep track of appointments and share them with family members who help coordinate care.

📈 WEEKLY REPORTS
See trends over time with comprehensive weekly wellness summaries.

🆘 EMERGENCY BUTTON
Hold for 2 seconds to call 911 in an emergency. Designed to prevent accidental calls while remaining accessible.

🔒 PRIVACY FIRST
Seniors control what they share. No surveillance, no tracking — just simple wellness communication.

📱 EASY TO USE
Large buttons, clear text, and senior-friendly design make this app accessible for everyone.

---

HOW IT WORKS

1. Senior downloads app and creates profile
2. Family members can connect and receive updates
3. Senior taps "I'm Okay" to send wellness check-in
4. Family sees wellness score and activity updates
5. Everyone stays connected without invasive monitoring

---

PERFECT FOR

• Seniors living independently
• Long-distance family caregivers
• Anyone who wants to check in on elderly parents
• Families who want peace of mind without surveillance

---

This app does NOT provide medical advice. Always consult healthcare professionals for medical decisions.
```
